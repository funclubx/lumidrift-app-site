LumiDrift is operated by Guangzhou Leqi Trading Co., Ltd. This policy explains the current v1.0 privacy practices for the LumiDrift iOS app.

If a future version adds ads, analytics, subscriptions, or cloud features, this policy will be updated before or when those features are released.
